Workplace Fire Safety

Workplace fires can be devastating to businesses and dangerous to employees. Proper fire safety measures, employee training, and regular maintenance can prevent most workplace fires and minimize damage when they occur.

Exit Safety

Keep exits clear and well-marked. Ensure emergency lighting works and exit signs are illuminated.

Equipment Maintenance

Regularly inspect and maintain electrical equipment, heating systems, and fire safety equipment.

Employee Training

Train employees on fire prevention, evacuation procedures, and proper use of fire extinguishers.

Chemical Storage

Store chemicals properly in approved containers and locations. Keep safety data sheets accessible.
