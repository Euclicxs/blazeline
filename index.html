Safety Resources

Home Fire Safety

Essential tips for preventing fires in your home, including smoke detector maintenance, electrical safety, and escape planning.

  • Test smoke alarms monthly
  • Keep fire extinguishers accessible
  • Create and practice escape plans
  • Never leave cooking unattended
Learn More

Fire Extinguisher Guide

Learn the proper types of fire extinguishers for different fires and how to use them effectively with the PASS technique.

  • Know your extinguisher types (A, B, C, D, K)
  • Remember PASS: Pull, Aim, Squeeze, Sweep
  • Check pressure gauges regularly
  • Replace or service as needed
Learn More

Emergency Preparedness

Be ready for any emergency with proper planning, emergency kits, and communication strategies for your family.

  • Build a 72-hour emergency kit
  • Establish family communication plans
  • Know local evacuation routes
  • Stay informed about local hazards
Learn More

Workplace Safety

Ensure your workplace meets fire safety standards with proper exits, equipment maintenance, and employee training programs.

  • Keep exits clear and marked
  • Store chemicals properly
  • Maintain electrical equipment
  • Conduct regular fire drills
