Emergency Preparedness

Be ready for any emergency with proper planning, supplies, and knowledge.

Emergency Preparedness Planning

Being prepared for emergencies means having the right supplies, knowing what to do, and having a plan that works for your family. Preparation can significantly reduce the impact of disasters and emergencies on your life and property.

Emergency Kits

Build a 72-hour emergency kit with food, water, medications, flashlights, batteries, and important documents.

Communication Plans

Establish family communication plans with emergency contacts, meeting places, and ways to stay informed.

Evacuation Routes

Know your local evacuation routes and have multiple ways to leave your neighborhood in case of emergency.

Stay Informed

Sign up for emergency alerts, monitor weather conditions, and know your local hazards and risks.
