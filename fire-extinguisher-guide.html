Fire Extinguisher Types & Usage

Not all fires are the same, and neither are fire extinguishers. Understanding the different types of extinguishers and their proper use can save lives and property. The PASS technique is the universal method for operating extinguishers.

Class A Extinguishers

For ordinary combustibles like wood, paper, cloth, rubber, and many plastics. Look for the letter A on the label.

Class B Extinguishers

For flammable liquids like gasoline, oil, grease, and paint. Look for the letter B on the label.

Class C Extinguishers

For electrical equipment like appliances, tools, and computers. Look for the letter C on the label.

PASS Technique

Pull the pin, Aim at the base, Squeeze the handle, Sweep from side to side. Remember PASS for effective extinguisher use.
