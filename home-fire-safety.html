Protect your home and family with essential fire safety knowledge and preventive measures.

Essential Home Fire Safety

Home fires can start and spread quickly, which is why we need to be careful and educated when it comes to fire safety. Every second counts during a fire. Preparation can make the difference between life and death.

Smoke Alarms

Working smoke alarms reduce the risk of dying in a fire by 50%. Install them on every level of your home and test them monthly.

Escape Planning

Create and practice a home fire escape plan with two ways out of every room. Designate a meeting place outside.

Cooking Safety

Never leave cooking unattended. Keep flammable items away from the stovetop and turn pot handles inward.

Heating Safety

Keep space heaters at least 3 feet from anything that can burn. Never use extension cords with space heaters.
