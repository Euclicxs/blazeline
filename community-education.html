Empowering Our Community Through Education

Our Community Education division is committed to promoting fire safety awareness through engaging programs, hands-on training, and community outreach initiatives. We believe that education is the key to preventing fires and saving lives.

Our Community Education Programs

School Programs

Interactive fire safety education programs designed for students of all ages. Our programs are age-appropriate and aligned with educational standards.

  • Elementary school presentations
  • Middle school safety workshops
  • High school career exploration
  • Teacher resource materials

Community Workshops

Hands-on training sessions for community groups, senior centers, and neighborhood associations. Learn practical fire safety skills in a supportive environment.

  • Fire extinguisher training
  • Smoke detector installation
  • Escape route planning
  • Seasonal safety tips

Business Training

Comprehensive fire safety training for employees, managers, and business owners. Ensure your workplace is prepared for fire emergencies.

  • Employee fire safety training
  • Manager emergency response
  • Fire drill coordination
  • Evacuation procedures

Special Events

Fire safety presentations for community events, health fairs, and safety expos. Engage with our firefighters and learn about fire prevention.

  • Fire truck demonstrations
  • Safety equipment displays
  • Meet the firefighters
  • Photo opportunities
