Our Fire Prevention Services

Residential Inspections

Comprehensive home safety inspections including smoke detector placement, electrical safety, escape route planning, and fire extinguisher recommendations.

  • Smoke detector placement verification
  • Electrical system safety checks
  • Escape route evaluation
  • Fire extinguisher recommendations

Commercial Inspections

Detailed commercial property inspections ensuring compliance with local fire codes, building safety standards, and emergency preparedness requirements.

  • Fire suppression system checks
  • Emergency exit compliance
  • Sprinkler system verification
  • Fire alarm system testing

Code Compliance

Expert guidance on local, state, and federal fire safety codes. We help ensure your property meets all current safety requirements and regulations.

  • Local fire code compliance
  • State safety regulation adherence
  • Federal safety standard verification
  • Compliance documentation

Education & Training

Comprehensive fire safety education programs for schools, businesses, and community groups. We provide hands-on training and safety demonstrations.

  • School fire safety programs
  • Business safety training
  • Community education workshops
  • Fire extinguisher training
